Target Audience

Agriculturalists

These people are the crop farmers, livestock herders and fishermen who can benefit from weather updates to save their assets from weather damage, reduce risk and therefore increase their productivity

Locals

These are everybody in the local areas of Idaho who are working, travelling, or anyone who can benefit to know the update of the weather for their own purposes.
